What I feel symptoms:

Here you will find the most practical information regarding chlamydia symptoms for different genders and sexual orientations. Symptoms are your sensations, something that you feel due to the impairments caused by the disease. On the contrary, signs are objective features of the disease, something you see and not feel. They can be detected not only by you but also by the healthcare provider. Thus, signs are more reliable than symptoms (seeing is believing). But still, they are essential constitutes of the disease, with the help of which the healthcare provider makes further assumptions and conclusions
